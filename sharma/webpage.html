About Food

1.Litti-Chokha

  • The most iconic Bihari dish.
  • Litti is a round ball made of wheat dough stuffed with roasted gram flour (sattu) and cooked over coal or in a clay oven.
  • Chokha is a mashed mix of roasted eggplant, boiled potatoes, or tomatoes with mustard oil, garlic, and spices.
