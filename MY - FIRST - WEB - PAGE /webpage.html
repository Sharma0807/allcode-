about begusarai

Begusarai is a district in the Indian state of Bihar, known for its history and culture. It is located on the northern bank of the Ganges River and is the administrative headquarters of the Begusarai district. The district has a significant history, being part of the Munger District until 1972 when it gained its own district status.
